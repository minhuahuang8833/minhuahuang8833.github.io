Minhua Huang

Professor of Computer Science
Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi

My research focuses on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Natural Language Processing (NLP), specifically exploring affective computing and sentiment-aware dialogue systems. I also develop optimization algorithms using Parallel Genetic Algorithms (RC-PGA).
